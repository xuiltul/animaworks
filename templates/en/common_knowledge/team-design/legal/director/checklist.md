# Legal Director — quality checklist

Checklist for Legal Director to protect deliverable quality with or without machine.

---

## A. Consistency with prior review (carry-forward gate)

- [ ] Every Critical finding from the last review is listed
- [ ] Each Critical finding has status: resolved / partially resolved / open / worsened
- [ ] “Partially resolved” items include residual risk assessment
- [ ] Unchanged High findings from the prior review are enumerated on this version
- [ ] Checked whether any Medium finding from the prior review worsened on this version
- [ ] carry-forward tracker status updated for every row (zero silent drops)

---

## B. Clause coverage

- [ ] Read the full agreement (not only changed clauses)
- [ ] Confirmed no silent changes outside the marked amendments vs prior version
- [ ] Checked for unfilled placeholders (●●, etc.)
- [ ] Checked definition section (Art. 1 equivalent) for changes
- [ ] Confirmed schedules/exhibits exist and were reviewed

---

## C. Risk calibration

For each risk item:

- [ ] Risk level (Critical/High/Medium/Low) has legal basis (statute, case, guideline)
- [ ] “Acceptable” / “no further negotiation” items state why
- [ ] “Industry standard” claims cite concrete support (case, treatise, stats)
- [ ] Downgrades vs prior review state a reason
- [ ] No hand-waving with guesses like “counterparty has no reason to refuse”

---

## D. Contract-type checks

Pick items that apply to the agreement type:

### D-1. Share purchase (SPA) / M&A

- [ ] IP ownership (background / foreground)
- [ ] Indemnity (caps, baskets, time limits, calculation, wording risk)
- [ ] Reps & warranties scope and survival
- [ ] Non-compete / non-solicit scope and duration
- [ ] Closing conditions (payment method, timing, confirmations)
- [ ] Termination and survival after termination
- [ ] Consideration mechanics and adjustment clauses

### D-2. NDA / general commercial

- [ ] Confidentiality scope, duration, carve-outs
- [ ] Entire agreement and relationship to prior contracts
- [ ] Effort standard (best efforts / reasonable / commercially reasonable)
- [ ] Party identifiers (legal form, address, signatories)
- [ ] Forum and governing law
- [ ] Damages scope (direct, indirect, consequential)
- [ ] Anti-organized-crime clause

---

## E. Output quality

- [ ] No factual errors (names, entities, dates, amounts)
- [ ] Recommended action on every risk row
- [ ] Negotiation priorities stated
- [ ] Pre-sign vs post-sign issues separated
- [ ] Plain enough for non-lawyers

---

## F. Delegation quality

### Before Verifier / Researcher

- [ ] `audit-report.md` has `status: reviewed`
- [ ] `analysis-plan.md` is attached
- [ ] carry-forward tracker is up to date
- [ ] Verifier / Researcher can access contract text and prior reports
- [ ] Deadline set

### Handling feedback

- [ ] All Verifier / Researcher feedback reviewed
- [ ] Re-rating where optimism bias was flagged
- [ ] Researcher evidence reflected in `audit-report.md`
- [ ] Final carry-forward tracker update complete
