# Sales & Marketing Director — quality checklist

Quality checklist for the Director regardless of machine usage.

---

## A. Content QC (verifying Creator's draft-content.md)

- [ ] Purpose, target, and key messages from content-plan.md reflected
- [ ] Brand Voice (tone, prohibited expressions, terminology) compliant
- [ ] Appropriate CTA for the funnel stage (TOFU/MOFU/BOFU)
- [ ] Depth and wording suitable for target persona
- [ ] No factual errors (company names, numbers, dates)

---

## B. Compliance (second-level judgment)

- [ ] No exaggerated or misleading claims
- [ ] No pharmaceutical regulation violations (for medical/health content)
- [ ] No advertising regulation violations (unfair advantage claims)
- [ ] Email regulation opt-in requirements met (for email campaigns)
- [ ] No privacy issues
- [ ] Determined whether {COMPLIANCE_REVIEWER} review is needed

---

## C. Sales content (machine output verification)

- [ ] Proposal customization appropriate for target
- [ ] Differentiation points accurate and backed by evidence
- [ ] Competitor information current (check retrieval date)
- [ ] Battle card and objection handling factually grounded
- [ ] ROI calculation assumptions stated

---

## D. Pipeline management

### Campaign Pipeline Tracker

- [ ] All content plans' stages updated to current
- [ ] Stagnation (2+ weeks without change) identified
- [ ] Completed plans have effectiveness measurement planned
- [ ] Zero silent drops confirmed

### Deal Pipeline Tracker

- [ ] All deals' stages updated to current
- [ ] Stagnation (2+ weeks without change) identified with action decided
- [ ] Lost deals have reasons recorded
- [ ] CS Handoff deals have cs-handoff.md created
- [ ] Zero silent drops confirmed

---

## E. Delegation quality

### Before delegating to Creator

- [ ] content-plan.md `status: approved` confirmed
- [ ] Target, key messages, production instructions sufficiently clear
- [ ] Compliance notes included (if applicable)
- [ ] Deadline set

### Before instructing SDR

- [ ] Outbound target criteria clear
- [ ] Message tone and direction specified

### Before delegating to Researcher

- [ ] Research purpose and scope clear
- [ ] Deadline set

---

## F. Feedback handling

- [ ] All draft-content.md from Creator reviewed
- [ ] SDR lead-report.md integrated into Deal Pipeline Tracker
- [ ] {COMPLIANCE_REVIEWER} compliance review results reflected
- [ ] Researcher research-report.md utilized
- [ ] Campaign/Deal Pipeline Tracker final status updated
