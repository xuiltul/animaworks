# SDR — quality checklist

Quality checklist for SDR lead handling, nurturing, and community engagement.

---

## A. Lead qualification (BANT evaluation)

- [ ] All 4 BANT items rated (yes / unknown / no)
- [ ] Each rating has specific evidence
- [ ] Custom fields evaluated (if configured)
- [ ] Qualification result (Qualified / Nurturing / Disqualified) stated
- [ ] If Qualified, lead-report.md sent to Director

---

## B. Nurturing quality

- [ ] Message tone appropriate for target
- [ ] Personalization included (name, company, pain point reference)
- [ ] Opt-out mechanism stated
- [ ] Sending frequency appropriate (weekly or less as a guideline)
- [ ] Consistent with previous interactions

---

## C. Compliance first filter

- [ ] Email regulation: sending to opt-in confirmed recipients
- [ ] Email regulation: sender info and opt-out mechanism included
- [ ] No exaggerated claims
- [ ] No unsupported performance/results claims
- [ ] Director consulted if compliance concerns exist

---

## D. Community engagement

- [ ] Consistent with brand image
- [ ] No confidential information disclosed
- [ ] No aggressive competitor language
- [ ] Responses factually grounded (not guessing)

---

## E. Escalation judgment

- [ ] CS-related inquiries correctly classified
- [ ] CS escalation includes necessary background
- [ ] Reporting timing to Director appropriate
- [ ] Borderline cases not self-decided
