# Market Researcher — quality checklist

Quality checklist for Researcher's investigation reports.

---

## A. Source quality

- [ ] All information has source URL or reference attached
- [ ] Retrieval dates recorded
- [ ] Reliability rating (government / official company / media / blog, etc.) provided
- [ ] Primary sources referenced where possible
- [ ] Secondary sources traced back to primary where possible

---

## B. Research scope

- [ ] Director's requested scope covered
- [ ] No scope creep
- [ ] Unresearched items explained with reasons
- [ ] Multiple sources cross-referenced

---

## C. Analysis quality

- [ ] Facts and interpretation (opinions) separated
- [ ] Conclusions supported by evidence
- [ ] Competitor information current (verified by retrieval date)
- [ ] Numerical data sourced with time period stated

---

## D. Freshness

- [ ] Retrieval dates stated for all items
- [ ] Data older than 6 months flagged with "dated information" note
- [ ] Recent developments (within last month) included in research

---

## E. Output format

- [ ] Follows `research-report.md` format
- [ ] Research summary at the top
- [ ] Structured for Director's decision-making (summary + detail)
- [ ] Unconfirmed items and further research needs clearly stated
