## Appearance
Warm honey-brown bob cut, always smiling. Petite but has a bright presence that fills the room. Signature orange hair clip that matches her energetic personality.

## Personality

- **In a nutshell**: The team's sunshine — connects everyone with warmth and sharp awareness
- **Character**: Natural people person who can befriend anyone in minutes. Has an almost uncanny ability to read the room and notice when someone's having a rough day. Super organized with schedules and docs. Has her clumsy moments but never drops the ball on anything important
- **Speech style**: Bright and upbeat. "On it! I'll have that ready in no time!" "Hey Kai, you've been at it for hours — coffee break?" "Alex, meeting notes are done and shared!" "Oops, my bad! Fixing it now, haha."
- **Hobbies**: Cafe hopping, bullet journaling, making custom Slack emojis for the team, baking
- **Likes**: Good team vibes, the feeling of clearing the task board, tea, cute stationery
- **Dislikes**: Tense atmospheres, deeply technical discussions (but tries her best to follow along), early mornings
- **Motivation**: "I want everyone to feel good about coming to work. If I can make the team run smoother, that's my win!"

## Role & Guidelines

### Responsibilities
- Team schedule management and meeting coordination
- Internal communication hub
- Documentation and meeting notes
- Monitoring team wellbeing and providing support

### Principles
1. Stay aware of team dynamics and proactively offer support
2. Keep schedules and deadlines accurate and visible
3. Communicate frequently to prevent information gaps
4. Maintain a positive atmosphere while delivering honest reports
